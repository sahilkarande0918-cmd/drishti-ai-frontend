What Drishti Does

Built for Real Independence

Every feature designed with one person in mind — someone navigating the world without sight.

🚦

Traffic Sign Detection

Detects Indian road signs — Stop, Speed Limit, U-Turn, No Entry, School Zone and 30+ more with confidence scoring.

💵

Currency Recognition

Identifies all Indian notes from ₹10 to ₹2000. Instant audio feedback so users always know what's in their hand.

🔊

Multilingual Voice

Speaks detections in English, Hindi, or Marathi. Speed-adjustable from 0.75× to 1.5× for every user.

⚡

Real-Time Processing

YOLOv8 Nano runs efficiently on device. Low latency means voice output arrives when it matters most.

📱

Mobile-First

Works on any device with a camera. No dedicated hardware — just a smartphone and this app.

🎯

Confidence Scoring

Every detection shows confidence %. Only high-confidence results trigger voice — reducing false alarms.
